Welcome to Abhi's Django for everyone project!

I am an engineering manager by profession and manage an engineering team building an enterprise product. I have always wanted to build websites and host web applications on my own!
While I have tried building RoR projects in the past, wasn't sure about the popularity of the language and support. That is when I learnt about Python & Django. I came across DJ4E which is a wonderful site to learn Django by example. Dr. Charles R Severance has done a wonderful job in teaching the course. Unlike other online django resources, he not only explains how to get things working but also what happens behind the scenes to make things work.
